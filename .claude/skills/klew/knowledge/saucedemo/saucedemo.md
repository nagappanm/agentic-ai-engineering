1. `login.username` ‚Üê `standard_user`

## Conventions & traps

- **"Add to cart" is ambiguous.** The user-facing `getByRole('button', { name:
  'Add to cart' })` matches **6** buttons (one per product) ‚Äî a strict-mode
  violation. Per-item elements are disambiguated by their `data-test`
  (`add-to-cart-sauce-labs-backpack`). This is a *uniqueness* problem, **not** an
  accessibility gap ‚Äî the buttons are properly labelled.
- **Password field isn't a reliable `textbox` role.** `getByRole('textbox', {
  name: 'Password' })` is unreliable for `type=password`; use the unique
  `getByPlaceholder('Password')` instead.
- **Cart badge is role-less.** The count (`shopping-cart-badge`) is a bare
  generic node ‚Äî a genuine a11y gap (should be `role="status"`), so it drops to a
  test-id locator and is `a11y_flag`ged.
- **No product search** ‚Äî narrowing the grid to make "Add to cart" unique isn't
  available; scope by the per-item test-id instead.
